My Favorite City

My favorite city is Dumaguete City, Philippines. The main reason is not because of the beauty of its tourist spots, but because of the latter-day saints I met there when I was serving a full-time mission. They are approachable, welcoming, and friendly. That city might not be the best in terms of beauty and popularity but it holds a lot of precious memories that I always want to look back on.
